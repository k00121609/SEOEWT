The Ultimate Pre-Launch Checklist for WordPress Websites

Get the Guide!

To improve your chances of a positive outcome and, ultimately, success, it’s a good idea to have a road map of everything that needs to be done before a website sees the light of day. That’s exactly what you’ll get with this pre-launch checklist for WordPress websites: a step-by-step template that makes sure you don’t forget any crucial steps on the journey to launch. That way, your site  will be an instant success, with Launch Day a celebration!

In this guide, written by Nick Schäferhoff for Torque and sponsored by WP Engine, you'll get a checklist of items in the following areas:

  • Content
  • Design
  • Functionality
  • SEO
  • Marketing
  • Legal
  • Move site to new server
  • Prepare for launch
  • Backup and security
  • Launch
  • Organization

Start checking off those items today!
