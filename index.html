What, Where & Why of Creative Multimedia Programming at LIT, Limerick?


The title of this course is: "H. Dip in Science in Computing in Creative Multimmedia Programming." This postgraduate programme offers rewarding and challenging possibilities for learners interested in undertaking a conversion programme in the area of multimedia, and its application in social, media and enterprise environments. Given the scale of the IT sector in Ireland, and the current expansion of roles associated with the IT sector, it is projected that there will be healthy demand for talented Computing/ IT graduates over the next number of years. The aim of this 1 year conversion programme is to provide students with an academically challenging educational experience that will enable them to develop the knowledge base, professional attitudes, project management skills and industry awareness to serve both the multimedia computing industry and society. The course also aims to provide the student with the opportunity to engage in independent research that integrates concepts, issues and ethical principles that allow the student to question existing precepts.

WHAT IS THE LIT EDUCATIONAL PHILOSOPHY ?

LIT has a distinctive approach to Teaching and Learning known as the Active Learning philosophy. Whatever discipline area is offered the core methodology is active and practical; lab work, studio work, case studies, field visits, group assignments and more. This approach to teaching and learning is embedded in assessment strategies and campus development so that the learning environment reflects the identity of LIT. WHAT ARE THE INDIVIDUAL MODULES IN CREATIVE MULTIMEDIA PROGRAMMING ABOUT ?

Duration:52 Weeks, 20 to 25 hours per week (Daytime) Commencing early September 2017 (TBC)


Evolving Web Technologies

This Course Involves Skill Development in Web Development, the Principles of Multimedia, Interaction Design, Navigation, Usability Principles, Responsive Design, Using Current IT Architecture and Languages of HTML and CSS in the Creation of Releveant Websites.

Read More...

3D Computer Graphics

This Course is About 3D Graphics and Animation. The Emphasis is on Developing an Understanding of the Industry-Standard Software of 3DS Max, utilizing this in the Modelling and Creation of 3D Scenes.

Advanced Web Programming

Advanced Web Programming is Understanding and Applying Programming Techniques which can be used in the Development of the Server Side Scripting of Websites. Languages covered in this course include: Javascript, PHP, MySQL, Code Ignitor.



Project Management

This Project Management Course is about Learning of the Theory, Practise, Skills and Techniques Required of a Project Manager in the Workplace. Topics Covered include: Critical Path Analysis (CPA), Work Breakdown Schedule (WBS),Communications, Budgets and Risk Analysis.

Multimedia Database Systems and Design

This Database Course covers many aspects of Database Design. This includes the understanding and practice using Industry Standard Software Packages such as phpMyAdmin and Microsoft Access. Students are Introduced to Concepts and Techniques like Views/Index, Transactions, Big Data, ERD Diagrams, Normalisaiton and SQL.

Interaction Interface Design

Interaction Interface Design Involves a Creative and Business Like Approach to Desiging Websites/Apps for Customers. There is an Emphasis on the Pre-development Process in the Importance of Involving the Client and Users at Every Step of the Design Process. Industry Standard Software Packages are used such as Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Acrobat, Axure.
